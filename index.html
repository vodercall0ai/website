Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

I. Core Features & Operations Q1: What is Vodercall, and how does it help me book a restaurant? A: Vodercall is an AI-powered table reservation assistant app (AI Booking Agent). Simply enter your target restaurant name, select your desired date, time, number of guests, and any special requests on the app. Our AI will then make a live voice call directly to the restaurant in Hong Kong or Bangkok, converse with the staff, and secure your reservation. The entire process is fully automated, saving you from waiting in cross-border lines or stressing over failed restaurant bookings overseas. Q2: Do I need to download an app to use this service? Or can I operate it directly on a webpage? A: You need to download the "Vodercall" app. Once you register an account, you can easily submit your reservation requests. Q3: How long does the AI take to book a restaurant? When will I know the results? A: Once we receive your request, the AI will place the call within 5 to 15 minutes, provided the restaurant is currently open. As soon as the call connects and the booking is confirmed, you will receive an instant email confirmation and a status update within the app. Q4: What happens if I submit a request when the restaurant is closed? A: No worries! Our system includes a smart scheduling feature. If you submit a request during the restaurant's off-hours, the AI will automatically queue your request and place the call the moment the restaurant opens the next day. Q5: Are there any restaurants that Vodercall cannot book? A: Vodercall can help with any restaurant that accepts reservations via phone call with opening hours clearly shown. However, if a restaurant exclusively uses online booking platforms (e.g., official website booking systems, Google Forms, or those requiring credit card deposits to hold a table) or has completely stopped taking phone orders, our AI agent will not be able to process the request through a phone call. II. Traveler & Language Solutions Q6: I don't speak Cantonese or Thai. Can I still use this service? A: Yes, absolutely! This is our most core feature. International travelers and business professionals can input booking details in your own language, our AI Agent will automatically convert your instructions into the local language (Cantonese or Thai) to converse with the restaurant staff, completely breaking down language barriers or limitations during your trip. Q7: Can I use Vodercall to book restaurants in Hong Kong and Bangkok while I am still in somewhere overseas? A: Definitely. As long as you have internet access to download the Vodercall app, you can submit booking requests 24/7 from anywhere. You don't need to pay expensive international call fees or purchase a local SIM card; our AI voice system will handle by making local calls on the ground. Q8: Restaurant staff in Bangkok often have thick accents. Can the AI understand and communicate with them? A: Our voice AI models have undergone localization training. Whether it's Thai-accented English, standard Thai, or local Cantonese (Hong Kong local language), the AI can precisely comprehend and respond naturally to various questions from restaurant staff. Q9: How is Vodercall different from online platforms like Google Map, Chope, or Tripadvisor? A: Many traditional high-quality restaurants and local hidden gems (such as traditional hidden Dai Pai Dongs in Hong Kong, or local Michelin street food vendors and trendy seafood restaurants in Bangkok) are not listed on online booking systems; they only accept traditional phone reservations. Vodercall bridges this gap by calling these phone-only establishments, ensuring you don't miss out on local culinary highlights. III. Troubleshooting & Contingencies Q10: What happens if the restaurant is fully booked or my requested time slot is unavailable? A: When submitting your booking request, you can input your first and second choices for the dining date and time. If the restaurant staff indicates that your preferred slot is full, our AI Agent will dynamically negotiate for your second choice or check for the next closest available time slots during the call (e.g., if you asked for 7 PM but it is full, the AI will flexibly ask if 6:30 PM or 8 PM works). If it is completely sold out, the system will immediately send an email notification so you can consider other dining dates and time options to change your reservation. Q11: Can I use the AI to cancel or modify my reservation if my plans change? A: Yes! Simply find your booking record in our system and click "Modify Trip" or "Cancel Reservation." Our AI Agent will call the restaurant again to notify the staff, helping you be a polite and responsible traveler who avoids being a "no-show." Q12: Will the system give up if the restaurant's phone line is busy? A: No. If the line is busy or unanswered, Vodercall triggers an automatic retry mechanism. The AI will redial every 10 to 15 minutes, attempting up to 5 consecutive times. If it remains completely unreachable, your tokens will be refunded, and you will be notified via email. Q13: Whose name should I give when checking in at the restaurant? A: The AI Agent uses the exact English or Chinese name and contact phone number you provided in the app when applying for the reservation. Upon arrival, simply present your confirmation letter and check in under your registered name at the designated time. IV. Pricing, Privacy & Security Q14: Is this AI booking service free? How does pricing work? A: We offer 3 complimentary credits (Tokens) for each newly registered user to experience an AI-driven booking service. Each successful reservation will deduct 1 Token. Once your credits are used up, you can purchase single-booking tokens at an affordable rate or opt for our traveler-friendly "Tourist Pass Packages." Q15: Will I be charged or lose tokens if the AI calls but the restaurant has no seats? A: Absolutely not. We strictly follow a "No Booking, No Charge" policy. If the booking fails due to a fully booked venue, closure for renovation, or an unreachable line, your tokens will be fully returned to your account. Q16: Does the Vodercall service fee include the cost of the meal? A: No, it does not. Vodercall solely handles the phone reservation service. All food expenses, service charges, or minimum spending requirements at the venue must be paid directly to the restaurant during your visit. Q17: If a popular restaurant requires a deposit over the phone, can the AI pay it for me? A: Currently, our AI Agent cannot process credit card payments or deposits over the phone on behalf of users. If your selected restaurant requires a deposit, Vodercall does not accept phone reservation applications for it. Q18: Is my personal name and phone information safe with Vodercall? A: We take data privacy and security very seriously. Your personal details (such as names and phone numbers) are only used for the specified restaurant reservation. All data transfers are secured with high-grade SSL encryption, and we never sell your data to third-party advertisers. Q19: Can I ask the AI to call for things other than restaurants (e.g., cabs, hotels, massages)? A: Currently, our voice AI model is deeply optimized specifically for food and beverage reservation scenarios in Hong Kong and Bangkok to guarantee the highest success rate. Other booking scenarios (such as other services) will be launched in future updates. Stay tuned! Q20: How do I contact customer support if I run into issues? A: If you have any questions or encounter any issues with a reservation, you can click the "Contact Us" button at the bottom of our website or email us directly at support@vodercall.ai. Our online support team will assist you as soon as possible. V. User Flow & Token System Q21: Step-by-Step: How do I place a booking request on Vodercall? A: Our user flow is incredibly simple and intuitive: 1. **Search & Select**: Type in the name of the restaurant in Hong Kong or Bangkok, then select your desired date, time, and number of guests. 2. **Provide Details**: Enter the booking name and contact phone number you want to leave with the restaurant. 3. **AI Deployment**: Click "Confirm Booking." Our AI Voice Assistant will handle dialing the local phone call for you. 4. **Easy Confirmation**: The moment the AI successfully secures your booking; you will receive an instant email and a real-time notification on your dashboard. Q22: What exactly is a "Token" and how does it work? A: A Token is the digital currency used on Vodercall to deploy our AI voice assistant. **Every successful restaurant reservation costs exactly 1 Token**. Q23: Do tokens expire if I don't use them during my current trip? A: No worries, your purchased tokens do not expire! If you bought a 5-token package for your trip to Bangkok but only ended up booking 3 restaurants, the remaining 2 tokens will stay safely in your account. You can use them for your next getaway to Hong Kong or your next vacation next year. Q24: Can I track what the AI Assistant is doing after I submit my request? A: Yes, absolutely. Your user dashboard provides a real-time status tracker. You can see exactly whether the AI is "Queueing," "Dialing," "Negotiating with Staff," or if the booking is a "Success" or "Failure." We keep the entire automation process completely transparent, allowing you to stay updated. Q25: Can I use 1 token to book multiple tables or a large banquet? A: One token covers one distinct calling session until a reservation is secured for your party size. If you are booking a large group (e.g., 10+ people) or require a special private room setup, you can specify this in the "Special Requests" field before deploying the AI so it can coordinate with the restaurant. However, if you need to book multiple separate tables under different dates or names, each individual reservation will require its own token.
