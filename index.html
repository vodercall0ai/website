Frequently Asked Questions | Vodercall
Vodercall is an AI-powered table reservation assistant app (AI Booking Agent). Simply enter your target restaurant name, select your desired date, time, number of guests, and any special requests on the app. Our AI will then make a live voice call directly to the restaurant in Hong Kong or Bangkok, converse with the staff, and secure your reservation. The entire process is fully automated, saving you from waiting in cross-border lines or stressing over failed restaurant bookings overseas.
