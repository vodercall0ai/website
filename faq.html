Frequently Asked Questions | Vodercall

I. What can Vodercall do for me?

Vodercall is an AI-powered table reservation assistant app (AI Booking Agent). Simply enter your target restaurant name, select your desired date, time, number of guests, and any special requests on the app. Our AI will then make a live voice call directly to the restaurant in Hong Kong or Bangkok, converse with the staff, and secure your reservation. The entire process is fully automated, saving you from waiting in cross-border lines or stressing over failed restaurant bookings overseas.
You need to download Vodercall at App store . Once you register an account, you can easily submit your reservation requests.
Once we receive your request, the AI will place the call within 5 to 15 minutes, provided the restaurant is currently open. As soon as the call connects and the booking is confirmed, you will receive an instant email confirmation and a status update within the app.
No worries! Our system includes a smart scheduling feature. If you submit a request during the restaurant's off-hours, the AI will automatically queue your request and place the call the moment the restaurant opens the next day.
Vodercall can help with any restaurant that accepts reservations via phone call with opening hours clearly shown. However, if a restaurant exclusively uses online booking platforms (e.g., official website booking systems, Google Forms, or those requiring credit card deposits to hold a table) or has completely stopped taking phone orders, our AI agent will not be able to process the request through a phone call.

II. How to use Vodercall to reserve restaurants abroad

Our user flow is incredibly simple and intuitive:

  1. Search & Select: Type in the name of the restaurant in Hong Kong or Bangkok, then select your desired date, time, and number of guests.
  2. Provide Details: Enter the booking name and contact phone number you want to leave with the restaurant.
  3. AI Deployment: Click "Confirm Booking." Our AI Voice Assistant will handle dialing the local phone call for you.
  4. Easy Confirmation: The moment the AI successfully secures your booking; you will receive an instant email and a real-time notification on your dashboard.
A Token is the digital currency used on Vodercall to deploy our AI voice assistant. Every successful restaurant reservation costs exactly 1 Token.

One token covers one distinct calling session until a reservation is secured for your party size. If you are booking a large group (e.g., 10+ people) or require a special private room setup, you can specify this in the "Special Requests" field before deploying the AI so it can coordinate with the restaurant. However, if you need to book multiple separate tables under different dates or names, each individual reservation will require its own token.
