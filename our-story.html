Our Story

VoderCall was born from a simple frustration: making a restaurant reservation abroad shouldn't require a local phone number, fluency in another language, or the courage to navigate an unfamiliar phone call.

We built VoderCall to solve exactly that. Our AI agent makes the call on your behalf — speaking the local language, handling the back-and-forth, and confirming your booking so you don't have to.

What started as a solution for travellers has grown into a platform used by people everywhere who simply want a smarter, more effortless way to reserve a table. We are a small, passionate team committed to making every booking seamless — no matter where in the world you are headed.
