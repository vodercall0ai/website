VoderCall was born from a simple frustration during a road trip in Normandy, France in 2024. We were driving to Le Havre for a short stop and were looking for a nearby restaurant. Thanks to Google Maps, we found a few options, but soon realized that booking a table required a local phone number, and there might be a long wait if we tried our luck as walk-in customers. On top of that, the language barrier made an unfamiliar phone call even more challenging.

That moment sparked an idea.

In this agentic era, it is good time to build an AI-powered solution for travellers facing the same challenge—people who simply want a smarter, more effortless way to reserve a table.

With VoderCall, our AI agent calls on your behalf, speaking the local language, handling the entire conversation, and confirming your reservation. Just submit your booking request, we'll take care of the rest and keep you informed of booking status.
